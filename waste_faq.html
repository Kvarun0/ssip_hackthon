Q: What is the strength of recycled paver block ?
A: It gives strength up to M35-M40; also you can use them hassle-free in low load-bearing structures.
Q: What amount of waste is used in final products ?
A: 84% of concrete waste is used in final products.
Q: Where we can use recycled ingredients ?
A: It can be used in concrete having grades less than M15, road sub-base, maintenance work, in construction of low load-bearing structures.
