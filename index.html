About Us

According to C&D waste rules 2016, it is mandatory to dispose the Concrete waste in a proper manner, and keep the surrounding clean. With this purpose, the Concrete Waste Recyclers aims to take care of this Concrete waste and convert it into useful recycled ingredients and final products. The Recycling Debris Collection Bank have a proper scientific facility for the segregation, crushing, separation, mixing, moulding of final product. Recycled ingredients and products will reduce the mining for aggregates and save the valuable land which will be wasted by dumping of the waste.

List Of Recycled Ingredients & Products :
  • Recycled Sand
  • Recycled Aggregates
  • Bricks
  • Paver blocks
  • Kerbs
  • Crash Barriers
